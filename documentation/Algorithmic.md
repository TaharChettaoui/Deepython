## Complexity chart
$f(n)$ is $O(g(n))$ means that $f(n)$ is bounded above by $g(n)$ asymptotically  
=> There exist constants $c>0$ and $n_0≥0$ such that $0≤f(n)≤c⋅g(n)$ for all $n≥n0$
